Please let me explain it better.

We do not want a futuristic high tech design.

The idea is that the website itself looks like it was physically 3D printed.

The whole interface should feel like real printed plastic: visible layer lines, soft plastic texture, raised and embossed elements, beveled edges, buttons that look printed, icons that look slightly extruded, and a clean modular structure.

It should still look like a modern professional website template, but with a 3D printed aesthetic.

Not a poster.
Not a full 3D scene.
Not a futuristic sci fi banner.
Not just an image inside a website.

The page design itself should look like a real 3D printed website UI.
